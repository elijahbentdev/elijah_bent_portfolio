Tri-Cleaner Python Plugin For Autodesk Maya

Tri-Cleaner converts radial triangle caps—commonly found on the tops of cylinders—into clean, quad-based geometry without altering the shape. Ideal for modeling workflows that require all-quad topology, it streamlines cleanup and prepares your mesh for subdivision, deformation, and export.

Bevel Buddy Python Plugin for Autodesk Maya

Bevel Buddy is your smart edge beveling assistant for Maya. It intelligently filters your edge selection and applies clean, customizable bevels with just a click. With powerful controls and a sleek dark-mode UI, it's the fastest way to get production-ready bevels — no cleanup required.
